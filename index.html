About the classes

The classes are held once a week. We work on one project for the full class term (This is called a crochet along or CAL for short). Each week you will receive a section of the pattern and we will discuss terminology and practice some of the trickier stitches and pattern repeats in class. You then take your project home and work on it for the week.

If you come across any issues or questions before the next class, you can reach out on the pattern support page for your class. You will get access to this page when you sign up. It is a great resource and often other members of the group will also step in and help with questions and share articles or videos they have found helpful. I find that a great feeling of community grows in the class with everyone working together and giving each other tips and tricks. The classes are enjoyable and social. I provide tea and coffee and try to create a relaxed and welcoming environment so that we can all learn together and grow some friendships.

You can find links to the projects for each class in the classes page of this website and a description of yarn and needles needed.
